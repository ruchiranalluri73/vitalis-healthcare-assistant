About Vitalis

Two trained models. One clear answer.

Vitalis doesn't guess — it runs your data through scikit-learn models that were already trained and evaluated on real clinical datasets, then uses AI only to translate the result into a plan you can actually act on.

Features

Built for clarity, not clutter

Heart disease screening

13 clinical markers — from resting blood pressure to ST depression — run through a locally-stored classifier trained on cardiac patient data.

Diabetes risk detection

Glucose, BMI, insulin and more are scaled and scored by a gradient boosting model, returning a confidence-backed prediction in seconds.

AI-guided recommendations

Once your result is in, Gemini builds a personalized diet plan, exercise routine, and lifestyle guidance — never the prediction itself.

How it works

From symptoms to a plan, in four stages

Input

Fill in your health markers

Choose a heart or diabetes screening and enter only the values the model was trained on — nothing more.

Local inference

The .pkl model predicts locally

Your inputs are scaled with the matching StandardScaler and passed straight into the trained classifier — no external API involved in the prediction itself.

AI recommendation

Gemini builds your plan

The result and risk level are sent to Gemini, which returns a diet plan, exercise routine, and lifestyle tips tailored to that outcome.

Follow-up

Ask the AI assistant anything

Keep the conversation going on the chatbot page for general health questions related to your result.
